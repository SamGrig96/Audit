WEEKLY DON PHARMACY SPO


1 = YES           2 = NO            3 = N/A 

1

MEDICATION ORDERS – Are orders properly documented? D/C dates and duration, prescriber, route.

2

STOCK MEDICATIONS –Have PAR levels been established for the site? Review Perpetual Inventory Forms. Patient specific meds must be stored separate from stock inventory.





3

MEDICATION CARTS –Are the medication carts clean and organized?

Conduct cart checks for any expired medications and defaced medications.





4

INJECTABLES- Are vials being dated and initialed when opened? (i.e. Normal Saline, Flu Vaccine, Heparin, Lidocaine, and PPD solutions) should be discarded after 30 days of opening





5

REFRIGERATED MEDICATIONS – Are open vials of meds being dated? (i.e. Insulins for 28-31 days)





6

REFRIGERATOR LOG – Are the refrigerator logs being completed? Refrigerator must be defrosted & cleaned monthly, verify that refrigerator is being used for meds ONLY.





7

PREPARING OF MEDICATIONS – Are there 2 identifiers on the pill envelopes? (Name & booking number or name & date of birth) medications, dosage, and route on the envelope.

Should only be used for lockdown units, 2nd tier or anywhere medication carts can’t be pushed through.

8

MARS – Are the nurse’s initialing when medication is administered? No Blanks? Are signatures of all nurses on the bottom MAR?

9

MARS - Is the prescriber’s name in the medication box along with the nurse’s initial that transcribed the order?

Is the duration of the order written on the MAR?

Are expired orders highlighted once complete?

10

EMR CHART REVIEWS – Problem Lists, Orders noted when necessary. Are nurses using correct forms for Medical Emergency? Medications being documented on MAR?

11

NARCOTICS – Is the count correct on working and bulk narcs? All signatures must be legible. Counts should be done at change of shifts with 2 signatures. BLACK ink only. Spot count working and bulk narcotics.

12

NARCOTICS DISPOSALS- Are we using reverse distributor? Are the narcotics that have to be destroyed properly labeled?

13

WASTED NARCOTICS – Are there 2 signatures for the waste?

Must be documented on MG-24 form

14

NARCOTIC MANIFESTS – Is there a narcotic manifest binder available? Receiving narcotics requires 2 signatures. Please review.

15

RECEIVING OF MEDICATIONS – Are medications reconciled daily? (Diamond’s Recon, Sapphire or CorEMR) Please review medication storage areas.

16

OPEN BOTTLES OF MEDICATION -Are meds being used within the expiration date? Medications must be given from original bottles.

17

REFUSALS –Are refusal forms completed in its entirety? Patients that are non-complaint with meds must be referred for counseling.

18

PRN MEDICATION – Are PRN medications properly documented?

19

INTERNAL/EXTERNAL MEDICATIONS - Are internal & external medications separated? ( i.e. topical medications cannot be stored with oral medications)

20

TREATMENT SUPPLIES – Is there date and time on open bottles of sterile water/normal saline? Only good for 24 hours once opened.

Hydrogen peroxide and alcohol only good for 30 days once opened.
