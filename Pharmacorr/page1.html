Medication Management

H-3 Are new orders sent to the pharmacy in a timely manner via fax or electronically?
H-4 Are manifest comments being reviewed and addressed appropriately?
H-5 New orders are reconciled against the manifest to find outstanding orders

H-12 Tickler file, offender request, or other system is in place to ensure refills are obtained when needed.

H-14 Does the nurse use the MAR to determine what meds to pass?
H-15 Did the nurse mark the MAR as they were passing the meds?
